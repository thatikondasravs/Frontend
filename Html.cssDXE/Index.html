OVERVIEW

What is a structural warranty or builders warranty?

A structural warranty , alsoknown as builders warranty, is agreement between a builder
and homebuyer that helps protect both the builder and homebuyer againist possible
construction defects. It sets clear expectations on both parties about what is covered and
what isn't covered in the builders warranty. Having structural warranty can significantly
reduce financial risks for both parties.
